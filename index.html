Smiski, a series of small, glow-in-the-dark collectible figurines created by Dreams Inc., a Japanese company.

Smiskis are said to live quietly in the corners of rooms, often hiding in plain sight while observing everyday life. Each series features Smiskis in different themes and activities, making them a fun surprise for collectors and fans of cute, whimsical characters. Made from phosphorescent material, Smiskis absorb light during the day and emit a soft green glow in the dark. Whether displayed on a desk, bookshelf, bedside table, or workspace, Smiskis bring personality and a sense of fun to any environment.
